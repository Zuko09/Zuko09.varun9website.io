What I do

I'm a 2nd-year Ph.D. student in the ECE dept at UC San Diego specializing in Machine Learning and Data Science.

I work with Edward Wang and Benjamin Smarr on applications of deep learning in medical and ubiquitous systems.

I graduated from the Paul G. Allen School for Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Washington in 2019, where I was advised by Dr. Shwetak Patel as part of the Ubicomp lab. There, I worked on a mix of research and engineering projects with the goal of adapting medical tools to smartphone devices
